Ramon Gilabert, Human Interface Designer from Barcelona.

Ramon Gilabert

Ramon is a passionate human interface designer that wishes to contribute to humanity by creating beautiful experiences that delight people. Originally from Catalonia, and having worked in Norway in the past, he’s currently working out of his studio in Barcelona.

In the past, Ramon has been lucky enough to collaborate with companies such as Microsoft, Red Bull, Domino’s, IKEA, Varner and Intersport, and has won internationally recognized awards and prizes for his work in the field of interaction and visual design.

For inquiries, please reach out to his email, or follow him directly on Twitter, where he regularly posts updates on his work, new projects he’s working on, and other design related topics.
