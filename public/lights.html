Lights

Name. Lights_

Client. Thesis_

Span. 7 months_

The project

Merging Electronics with a beautifully designed product to create an easy to use solution to control an elegant 3D designed lamp_ Two servers, one iOS application and one micro-controller for the light are some of the components that were born out of this project.

Wireframing

The initial idea was clear. Present simplicity over a light color to show contrast with the actions the user needs to take. Go back to a tiny skeuomorphic solution and try ripples and action showers.

Attempting

Tried to keep the fun level of the app as high as possible, having in mind that everything needed an animation or a transition. Started playing with some icon families that showed consistency.

Going Black

After some attempts with white, beige, clear creme and even gray I thought, – Let's try a dark interface instead. The colors of the picker would have to be sharper and with a more neon style, so it wouldn't be as flat as we are used to. But that was all right. The details of the app would have more consistency and strength over a dark color.

My biggest side project to this date. I was asked to think creatively. So I went nuts. After 7 months working every afternoon after work. I delivered this award winner thesis.
