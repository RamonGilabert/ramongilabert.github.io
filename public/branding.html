Name. Branding_

Client. Ramon Gilabert_

Span. 3 months_

The project

My most personal project up to this date. Working against the clock to do the design, photography, concept, development and testing. After pages of drawings and sketches, though, here it is. The project that has defined my style.

R&G

I started the project with the wrong mindset. Finish the logo first, then do the rest. I had some guidelines, so I started drawing. I draw over 300 different logos with shapes and combinations of my initials. None of them convinced me at all.

Restarting

After pages and logos that didn’t go anywhere. I stopped. Centered my efforts on the resumé, business card, etc. and start using a placeholder for my logo instead. This is when I started to see the direction of the style. Afterwards, the logo would made sense in the context of the whole branding.

Glitching

I remember when I told my friend: – I got it. I know the direction of this. I started watching then videos with crazy sound and glitches in the internet to get inspiration, there it was. Simple, clean and beautiful. I know I would have to create tons of different glitches to make it personal in every occasion. I was ready. And this is the result of it.

After nights working, sketches, prints papers scratched and lots of hours of watching old TVs and failing printers I found the style that now defines me. It’s been a long process, but I’m super satisfied with the result of it.
