The story

Originally from a small town in rural Catalonia and after +1.5 years in Norway working as an iOS developer & Concept Designer, while graduating from Electronic Engineering, I’m up for new challenges and new stories.

The manifesto

When I get asked to describe the work I do, I keep on thinking. I do what makes you think. The prototype that makes you win. The design that makes you wow. Welcome to my site. I’m Ramon. It’s nice to have you here.

The awards
